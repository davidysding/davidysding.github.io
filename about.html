    I Love Reading!

    Here are some top books I read that I would like to share with you!

    An Apple A Day by Joe Schwarcz Digital Storytelling by Carolyn Handler Miller America Alone by Mark Steyn How To Write Short by Roy Peter Clark

    An Apple A Day by Joe Schwarcz is a great book to read if you would like to eat health and scientific!

    The book tells you about the myths, misconceptions and truth about the food and thing you learned from the commercials you watch on media!

    Digital Storytelling by Carolyn Handler Miller. An excellent and must-read for writer in the interactive industry. The book teaches the reader how to write in different interactive media, such as video game, mobile app, interactive cinema etc..

    Definitely, it is a very detailed and useful handbook on the desk.

    America Alone by Mark Steyn. The book criticize the current welfare system, culture, politics, religion and multi-culturalism. It makes me deeply rethink about the good sides and the down sides of our system and the values we are told by education.

    How To Write Short by Roy Peter Clark. This is a relatively easy-read and a guidebook that teaches the reader the required skills to write brief and concise. It also lists many classical pieces of writings that does not excess 300 words.

    This is also a book should be put on the "workbench".
